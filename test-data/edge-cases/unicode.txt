International researchers collaborate on the Global AI Initiative.

Dr. François Müller from ETH Zürich specializes in natural language processing for European languages. His email is francois.mueller@ethz.ch and website is https://ethz.ch/de/forschung/müller.

Prof. 田中優子 (Yuko Tanaka) from Tokyo University works on Japanese semantic technologies. Her email is tanaka@u-tokyo.ac.jp and her research page is at https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/研究者/田中.

Dr. Björk Sigurðsson from the University of Iceland studies Nordic language processing. Contact: bjork.sigurdsson@hi.is with homepage https://hi.is/staff/björk.

Prof. Αλέξανδρος Παπαδόπουλος (Alexandros Papadopoulos) from the National Technical University of Athens researches Greek computational linguistics. Email: alexandros.papadopoulos@ntua.gr.

Dr. Анна Петрова (Anna Petrova) from Moscow State University works on Slavic language technologies. Her contact is anna.petrova@msu.ru and website https://msu.ru/сотрудники/петрова.

Prof. 李明 (Ming Li) from Tsinghua University specializes in Chinese knowledge graphs. Email: ming.li@tsinghua.edu.cn and research page at https://tsinghua.edu.cn/研究/李明.

Dr. José García-López from Universidad Complutense de Madrid studies Spanish semantic web applications. Contact: jgarcia-lopez@ucm.es with site https://ucm.es/investigación/garcía-lópez.

The group has published papers in multiple languages including "Représentation des connaissances multilingues" (French), "知識グラフの構築" (Japanese), and "Семантическая паутина" (Russian).

Their collaborative project "Πολύγλωσση Γνώση" (Multilingual Knowledge) aims to create interoperable knowledge systems across language barriers. The project website is https://multilingual-knowledge.org/über-uns.
